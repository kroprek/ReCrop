Melisapp highlights


Precision Beekeeping (or Precision Apiculture) aims to help beekeepers monitor bee colonies remotely and identify different colony states including deviant behaviour.
  1. Swarming
  2. Colony absconding
  3. Queen status
  4. Colony strength
  5. Foraging Activity
